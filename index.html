Category Theory

1. Categories are an abstraction of mathematical concepts.

2. It is alternative to Set Theory within Mathematics.

3. It Focuses on relationships between objects.

Lambda Calculus

1. Lambda Calculus may be typed or untyped.

2. The foundation of Lambda Calculus is the "expression".

3. Functions can be applied to other functions

The Curry Howard Isomorphism

The Curry-Howard Isomorphism is also known as the Curry-Howard correspondence. It describes the relationship between computer programmes and mathematical proofs. It uses Hilbert-style deduction and combinatory logic. It is the foundation of the proof system used for functional programming. It lays the groundwork for type systems and intuitionistic/ constructive logic as proofs for such languages.
